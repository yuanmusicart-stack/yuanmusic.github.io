About Us

Love is the beginning of music. Yuan Music is a haven amidst the fast-paced life of Silicon Valley—a small oasis where aesthetic beauty and professional excellence meet.

We are dedicated to nourishing children’s hearts with art and providing a mindful space for adults to slow down, rediscover their inner peace, and find harmony within a busy world.

Mission & Vision

We provide professional private music education alongside innovative school programs, making art effortlessly accessible for active households.

Giving Back

Giving back is a core part of our values. We commit 10% of our profits to supporting local churches and non-profit organizations.

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give... for God loves a cheerful giver." (2 Cor 9:7)
