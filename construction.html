About Us

Yuan Music is more than a studio; it is a community where talent meets purpose. We believe that music is a divine gift used to uplift souls and connect people.

Mission & Vision

To cultivate the next generation of musicians through professional excellence and Christian values.

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give... for God loves a cheerful giver." (2 Cor 9:7)

We commit 10% of our profits to local churches and non-profit organizations.

Our Programs

Private Piano Classes

One-on-one professional instruction tailored to each student's journey, focusing on technique, theory, and the joy of worship through music.

After-school Programs

We bring music directly to your school. By integrating our programs into the school day, we help busy families reduce commute times and strengthen the bond between students and their school communities.
