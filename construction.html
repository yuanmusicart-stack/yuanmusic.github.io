About Us

Love is the fountainhead of music. Love carries warmth—much like an oasis, it offers nourishment, solace, connection, and confidence. We are dedicated to nurturing a deep love for music and art within our students, allowing them to experience the beauty of artistry and the warmth of life amidst a fast-paced world.

Mission & Vision

Yuan Music offers private piano lessons throughout the Fremont, Union City, and Newark areas in the Bay Area. We also partner with local schools to bring high-quality music programs directly to students, aiming to ignite a lifelong passion for music and inspire true artistry.

Giving Back

Giving back is a core part of our values. We commit 10% of our profits to supporting local churches and non-profit organizations.

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give... for God loves a cheerful giver." (2 Cor 9:7)
