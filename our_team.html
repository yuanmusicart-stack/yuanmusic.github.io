Meet Our Professional Instructors

Ms. Zhang

Music Director | Piano Instructor

Yuanyuan has been a dedicated pianist since age five. After earning her Ph.D. in Materials Science and spending a decade as a Senior Scientist in the Bay Area, she founded Yuan Music in 2026 to harmonize her dual passions. This rigorous scientific foundation allows her to articulate the structural and mathematical beauty of music with unique clarity. Through a "Scientist-Artist" perspective, she deconstructs complex compositions with analytical precision while infusing them with deep emotional resonance. With professional performance certificates and advanced studies at the San Francisco Conservatory of Music, Yuanyuan mentors students aged 4 to 65+. A patient and intellectually refined mentor, she is dedicated to nurturing technical mastery alongside each student's personal artistic growth. She also serves as a piano accompanist for the HOC6 choir.
