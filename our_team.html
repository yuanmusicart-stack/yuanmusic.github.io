Meet Our Professional Instructors

Ms. Zhang

Music Director | Piano Instructor

A dedicated pianist since age five, Yuanyuan served as a principal piano accompanist during her university years in Shanghai before moving to the U.S. to earn her Ph.D. in Materials Science. After a decade in biotech/biophamaceutical companies in the Bay Area, she founded Yuan Music in 2026. Yuanyuan holds professional certificates in Piano Performance and has completed advanced studies in Music Theory and Appreciation at prestigious Bay Area institutions. Teaching students of all ages (4-65+), she leverages her unique "Scientist-Artist" perspective to blend technical precision with deep emotional expression. She is a patient mentor committed to nurturing each student's artistic and personal growth. She also serves as a piano accompanist for the Home of Christ Church (HOC6) choir
